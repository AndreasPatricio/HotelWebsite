About Azure Mall

Azure Mall was builded on 1999 and is one of the biggest mall in Pekanbaru with a blue or azure colour concept that will surely make you enjoy going here. Inside Azure Mall there is a lot of shopping center, restaurant, and many more entertainment place for you to enjoy your hobbies. Azure Mall aim to be a one stop place for all you ever needs.

Azure Mall is located at Jeruk Street with an area of 100.000 m2 and 5 floors 1 basement. This mean Azure Mall can acommodate almost 1000 person at once. Not just that Azure Mall also always do unique events every month , that means every day will always be a memorable memories for you
